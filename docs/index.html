Inspiration

Urithiru is named for the legendary city in the Stormlight Archive—a sanctuary, a nexus, a place of light above the storms. Our little version is a nod to that spirit: refuge, ritual, and a touch of wonder.

Location

Tucked in the basement of Chateau Knapp, Urithiru is a cozy, low‑light retreat—geometric accoutrements, clean lines, and the quiet hum of glassware.

Adjacent to the Theater

Steps from the theater room, this is the perfect intermission bar—martinis before the feature, amaro after the credits. Dim lights, crisp sound, and a door away from the next scene.
